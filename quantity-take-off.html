QUANTITY TAKE-OFF

We undertake to define exactly the materials requirement to be used, the quality criteria and how they should be processed and installed.

  • - Preparation of general layout drawings for quantity take-off.
  • - Preparation of summary of orders for all necessary materials.
  • - Checking of the design technical data submitted for the materials for Architect or Engineer's approval.
  • - Comparison of the materials proposed by the contractor with the specified materials and corresponding waste.
  • - Preparation of materials cutting list.
